Publications


My research focuses on streaming data mining and trustworthy AI. I am interested in providing robust online forecasting results for streaming data that is neither identically nor independently distributed. In addition, I am also keen on using the forecasting results to support real-time decision-making. So far, I have studied the real-time decision-making methodologies for energy sector.
